This is a page about some cool places to go.

Portland has a a lot of neat places, here are several.

  • Powell's City of Books

    A really big bookstore with a lot of books and pretty good prices.

  • Voodoo Donut

    Vodoo doughnuts, best known for their weird but delicious pastries.

  • The Waterfront

    A popular place in Portland where you will find lots of poeple throughout the day jogging, walking or just enjoying the sun.
